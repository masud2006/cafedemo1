Our Story

Welcome to Dine Dhaka

Established in 2018 in the heart of Dhaka, Dine Dhaka began as a small café with a big dream: to create a space where food, culture, and community intersect. Our founders, passionate about both Bangladeshi culinary traditions and global flavors, wanted to craft a unique dining experience.

Today, we're proud to be one of the city's most beloved destinations for food lovers. Our chefs source the finest local ingredients, combining them with innovative techniques to create dishes that delight the senses.

Whether you're here for a business lunch, a romantic dinner, or simply a perfect cup of coffee, we promise an unforgettable experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Bangladesh with a modern twist.
